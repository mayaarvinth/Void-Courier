# Void Courier

Welcome, Courier! You are the last line of defense in a fractured reality.

Your mission is simple: retrieve lost parcels from the Void and deliver them back to Earth.

## How to Play

**Controls**

Move Left / Right --> Arrow Left / Arrow Right or A / D

Jump --> Arrow Up / W or Space

Retry Level --> R

Navigate Menu --> Space / Enter

**Gameplay Objectives**

* **Collect the Mail:** Navigate through challenging terrain to pick up floating mail parcels (marked with a yellow "M").

* **Meet the Quota:** Each level has a specific quota of mail you must collect before you are allowed to enter the house and complete the stage.

* **Avoid Hazards:** Watch out for spikes and the encroaching Void wall.

* **Utilize Powerups:** Keep an eye out for items that grant you advantages like Double Jump, Speed Boost, Shields, and Mail Magnets.

    - Double Jump: Reach higher platforms.

    - Speed Boost: Move faster to outrun the Void.

    - Shield: Protect yourself from a single hit.

    - Mail Magnet: Automatically pull nearby mail towards you.

The Home Stretch: Once you have collected enough mail, find the house icon to complete the stage.

**Earth Mode:**

Once you have completed all of the levels, you can join the cozy game!

Use arrows to match mails to houses based on color.
